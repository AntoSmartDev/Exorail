# DECISIONS.md

## Purpose

Record accepted, proposed, deferred, and superseded project decisions. Initial setup owns the first entries.

| ID | Decision | State | Blocking | Scope | Evidence or rationale | Decision source | ADR |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Rule

- a proposed blocking decision prevents delivery readiness
- an accepted human decision records `user:<decision-reference>` as its actual source
- policy or process exceptions and material residual-risk acceptances record affected scope and concise rationale; the complete Human Decision Brief remains in chat
- use an ADR only when the decision needs durable alternatives and consequences
